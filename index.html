About Me

Hello there, I'm Matt!

I am a data scientist with a background in 3D art and video game design. Since I come from a background of story telling and art, I put an emphasis on understanding the interesing narratives that can be found in data and showing insightful trends through clear and beautiful visualizations. Over the past few years, I have also become increasingly interested in Futures trading. As I am on the Pacific Standard Time, I start every morning at 5am to prepare for the market open, analying chart data by hand. It became apparent that with data science, I could analyze larger amounts of data through code and utilize machine learning to optimize my trading strategies. With this hope in mind, I stepped into the world of data science and found many more doors and paths opening up to me than I had imagined, and now I am excited to start a career in data.
